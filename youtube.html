My Favorite YouTubers

3DMACHINES

This is my number one favorite YouTuber, 3DMACHINES. His real name is Dalton and he lives in New York State. I started watching him because he made videos of dirbikes, his duramax, and all the projects he works on. I've only been watching him for 4 months but he's become one of my favorite YouTubers. His videos brighten up my day and I look foward to watching his videos after I get home from school.

Street Speed 717

Street Speed 717 is a YouTuber named Mike and he lives in Pennsylvania. He has a Corvette Z06 and a Lifted Duramax truck. I started watching him due to his truck and his offroad videos. His Corvette looks factory, but it has over 700 horsepower and can beat most vehicles in a race including a Lamborghini.

NOVRITSCH

NOVRITSCH is my favorite airsoft YouTuber. He is from Austria and I do not know his real name. His content is very informative and explanatory. He mainly uses a Sniper Rifle in huge outdoor fields. NOVRITSCH travels the world to play airsoft at different places. Airsoft is a military simulator but in real life. The guns can be powered by co2, propane, or battery. They shoot plastic bbs and can be very realistic looking.
